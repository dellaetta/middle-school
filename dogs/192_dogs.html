The German Shepherd

The first German shepherd was found in 1899. German shepherds were bred to protect and herding wildstock. Today they are also used for guard dogs, guide dogs, and also sometimes work with police officers. Some of their traits are strong, loyal, courageous, and they focus. German shepherds also are intelligent and have a strong grip. They usually live around 10 to 12 years.
